Trending

Colors

Latest color trends of 2024 are soft blue, peach fuzz, terracotta & dusty pink, they merge well with textures like linen, wool & cotton. It harmonizes well with wood & stone too.

Wallpapers

Latest trend is to wallpaper every single wall of your room, go all out with solid colors & wallpapers. Accent walls have become outdated in 2024.

Flooring

Flooring trend in 2024 is using natural stones including granite, marble, limestones etc. Its not about the sleekness anymore rather the texture & porousness is in style.

Furniture

If you want to keep your furniture up-to-date, go for arches. When you pair them with paint you have your own master piece. Design it according to what suits you.
