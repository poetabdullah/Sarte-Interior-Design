Restaurants

Offices

Our client was longing for a serene environment for their office to work in. So we decided to use colors like white, grey & shades of skin. For the cupboards we used granite stone to exhibit strength. To add color to the space we used a navy blue rug under the table with a little off-white tone. We have also used slim LED lights to give an aesthetic touch.

- The Hive

As per our client's demand we have used the shades of brown, and kept the furniture minimalistic to make the space airy & breathing for our client. We have done wood flooring & marble cupboards to give it a contrast. We used LED slim lights to enhance the simplicity of the place.

- Infinity Place

Bedrooms

For the villa, we chose something that would make the room look auspicious & airy, we went for the bow window & used white color for the room, for the garden's greenery to give it life. Furniture was chosen to be red oak so the room looks exuberant. Paintings & the study table is annexed to use it as a workroom too.
